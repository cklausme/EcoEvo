FindExtrema

FindExtrema[f, {tmin, tmax}]
finds local extrema of InterpolatingFunction f between tmin and tmax.

FindExtrema[f]
looks over entire domain of f.

DetailsDetails

  • Based on a Mathematica.StackExchange idea from Daniel Lichtblau and a trick from Mr. Wizard
  • Result is a list of time values and extremum values.
