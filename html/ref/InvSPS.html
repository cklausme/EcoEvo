InvSPS

InvSPS[pops, inv]
calculates the growth rate and stable population structure of invader inv invading the resident community pops.

InvSPS[traits, pops, Guildguild]
calculates the growth rate and stable population structure of an invader in guild guild (default=first), using resident trait values traits.

InvSPS[traits, pops, traitinv]
uses invader traits traitinv.
