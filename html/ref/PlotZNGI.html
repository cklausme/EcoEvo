PlotZNGI

PlotZNGI[sp, {var1, var1min, var1max}, {var2, var2min, var2max}]
plots a zero net growth isocline of sp.

PlotZNGI[{sp1, sp2, …}, {var1, var1min, var1max}, {var2, var2min, var2max}]
plots multiple ZNGIs.

PlotZNGI[traits, {var1, var1min, var1max}, {var2, var2min, var2max}]
plots ZNGIs with traits.

DetailsDetails

  • The following options can be given:
  • InvOpts{}options to pass to Inv
    DelayInvFalsere-evaluate Inv numerically at each point
    PlotType"ZIP"type of plot
    InvThreshold0threshold for successful invasion
    PlotOpts{MaxRecursion -> 3}options to pass to ContourPlot / RegionPlot
    PlotStyleAutomaticstyle of invasion boundary
    InvStyleOpacity[0]color to indicate positive growth
    NonInvStyleOpacity[0]color to indicate negative growth
    MonitorFalsepopup progress monitor window
    VerboseFalseprint extra info
    VerboseAllFalseapply Verbose recursively
