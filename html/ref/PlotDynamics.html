PlotDynamics

PlotDynamics[sol]
plots the functions in sol across their entire domain.

PlotDynamics[sol, funcs]
plots funcs in sol across their entire domain.

DetailsDetails

  • PlotDynamics uses Plot or LogPlot for ContinuousTime models and ListPlot or ListLogPlot for DiscreteTime models.
  • The following options can be given:
  • LoggedFalsewhether to log the y-axis
    JoinedTruewhether to join the points in DiscreteTime
    PlotStyle{}overrides default PlotStyle from model specification
    PlotMarkers{}overrides default PlotMarkers from model specification
    AxesLabelAutomaticlabels for x- and y-axes
  • In addition, any options for Plot or ListPlot can be used.
  • PlotRange is determined automatically based on the ranges in the model specification.
  • AxesLabel is automatically generated by default (turn off with AxesLabelNone).
