Avg

Avg[f, var]
gives the average of f with respect to var (default var=t).

Avg[f, {var, varmin, varmax}]
ranges from varmin to varmax.

Avg[rulelist]
threads over rulelist.

DetailsDetails

  • The following options can be given:
  • MethodAutomatichow to calculate average
    IntegrateOpts{}options to pass to Integrate
    NIntegrateOpts{}options to pass to NIntegrate
  • Possible explicit settings for Method include:
  • "Integrate"use Integrate (continuous-time)
    "NIntegrate"use NIntegrate (continuous-time)
    "Sum"use Sum (discrete-time)
  • By default, Avg uses Integrate for functions, NIntegrate for IntepolatingFunctions, and Sum for TemporalData.
