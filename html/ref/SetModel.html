SetModel

SetModel[model]
sets an EcoEvo model for analysis.

DetailsDetails

  • The following options can be given:
  • ColorsColorData[97,"ColorList"]List of Colors
    LineStyles{{}}List of PlotStyles
    PlotMarkersGraphics`PlotMarkers[]List of PlotMarkers
    Gradient"Rainbow"A ColorData Gradient
    Gradients{"EEGreens", "EEReds", "EEBlues"}A List of ColorData Gradients
  • The following model parts can be given:
  • ModelName"UnnamedModel"model name (String)
    ModelType"ContinuousTime""ContinuousTime" or "DiscreteTime"
    Period0 (unforced)0 (unforced), positive Real (periodic), or ∞ (aperiodic)
    ModelWhenEvents{}a list of WhenEvents
    AssumptionsAutomatica list of Assumptions
    Aux[aux] Name, Equation, Range, Color, LineStyle, PlotMarker
    Pop[pop] Name, Component[pco]
    Guild[gu] Name, Component[gco], Trait[tr]
    Component[co] Equation, Range, Type, Color, LineStyle, PlotMarker
    Trait[tr] Range, Color, LineStyle, PlotMarker
    Name"Aux"<>au, "Pop"<>pop, "Guild"<>guString
    Equation Dynamic equation (right-hand side, var'[t] or var[t+1])
    Range{0,∞} (component), {–∞,∞} (trait)Interval[]
    Type"Extensive"{"Extensive", "Intensive"}
    ColorColors optionColor
    LineStyleLineStyles optionLineStyle
    PlotMarkerPlotMarkers optionPlotMarker
  • Use Rule (-> / ) to define model parts, except use RuleDelayed (:> / ) to define Equations of Auxs and Pops.
  • "Extensive" population components are measured in terms of numbers or densities; "Intensive" population components are measured on a per individual basis. See https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Intensive_and_extensive_properties for more info. Default is "Extensive".
  • AssumptionsAutomatic sets inequalities for all Auxs and Pops and species 0-2 in Guilds based on their Ranges.
  • SetModel sets the following variables in EcoEvo`Private` context:
