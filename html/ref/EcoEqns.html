EcoEqns

EcoEqns[traits]
sets up ecological equations corresponding to species with traits.

DetailsDetails

  • EcoEqns is used in many EcoEvo functions. It usually doesn't need to be called by users.
  • The following options can be given:
  • Fixed{}fixed variables
    TimeScale1change of timescale
    LoggedFalselog transform the model
    PerCapitaFalsegive per capita rates on right hand side
    NonFixedVarsAutomaticlist of equations to return
