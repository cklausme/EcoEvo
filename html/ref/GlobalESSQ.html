GlobalESSQ

GlobalESSQ[traits, sol]
checks global ESS stability with traits and ecological attractor sol.

DetailsDetails

  • GlobalESSQ returns a list of two lists of results: first, the evolutionary stability (True/False) for each guild; second, the corresponding results of MaximizeInv.
  • The following options can be given:
  • MaximizeInvOpts{}options to pass to MaximizeInv
    InvThreshold10^-10threshold for successful invasion
    VerboseFalseprint extra info
    VerboseAllFalseapply Verbose recursively
