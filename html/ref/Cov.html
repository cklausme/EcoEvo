Cov

Cov[f1, f2, var]
gives the covariance of f1 and f2 with respect to var (default var=t).

Cov[f1, f2, {var, varmin, varmax}]
ranges from varmin to varmax.

Cov[rulelist]
threads over rulelist to give variances and covariances.

DetailsDetails

  • The following options can be given:
  • MethodAutomatichow to calculate covariance
    IntegrateOpts{}options to pass to Integrate
    NIntegrateOpts{}options to pass to NIntegrate
    AvgOpts{}options to pass to Avg
  • Possible explicit settings for Method include:
  • "Integrate"use Integrate (continuous-time)
    "NIntegrate"use NIntegrate (continuous-time)
    "Sum"use Sum (discrete-time)
  • By default, Cov uses Integrate for functions, NIntegrate for IntepolatingFunctions, and Sum for TemporalData.
