Trait-Based Lotka-Volterra Predator-Prey

The trait-based Lotka-Volterra predator-prey model adds a trophic level to the trait-based Lotka-Volterra competition model.

    

As in the trait-based Lotka-Volterra competition model, k[xni] describes the unimodal trait-dependence of the carrying capacity and a[xni,xnj] describes how the strength of competition depends on the difference between species traits, assumed to be a Gaussian function of the trait differences with width σ. In addition c[xni,xpj] describes how predation depends on predator and prey traits, another Gaussian function of the trait differences with width σc.
