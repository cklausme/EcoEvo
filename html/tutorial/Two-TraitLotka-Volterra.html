Two-Trait Lotka-Volterra

The two-trait Lotka-Volterra competition extends the trait-based Lotka-Volterra competition model to two traits.

    

k[xi,yi] describes the unimodal trait-dependence of the carrying capacity and a[xi,yi,xj,yj] describes how the strength of competition depends on the difference between species traits (assumed to be a bivariate Gaussian).
